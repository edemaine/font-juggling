Juggling Font

by Erik Demaine and Martin Demaine, 2015

This font is preliminary and we welcome your feedback and suggestions for improvement! Each letter is represented by a juggling pattern, shown as either an animation produced by the Juggling Lab software or as a single image of the ball trajectories (produced by overlaying the frames of the animation). It was prepared in honor of Ron Graham's 80th birthday.
